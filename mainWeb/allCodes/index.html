About Us – CGEC Times

Welcome to CGEC Times, the official newspaper club of Cooch Behar Government Engineering College. Established in 2022, we are dedicated to covering all major events, achievements, and activities happening across our campus.

As one of the largest student-led clubs in CGEC, we take pride in bringing well-researched, engaging, and timely news to our readers. Our team works collectively to publish a comprehensive monthly edition that highlights the spirit, innovation, and culture of our college.

We are privileged to have Assistant Professor Sourav Chakraborty as our mentor, guiding us in our journey to make an impact through journalism. With a passionate team and a commitment to quality storytelling, CGEC Times continues to be the voice of our campus

Stay informed. Stay inspired. Stay with CGEC Times!

About CGEC

Cooch Behar Government Engineering College (CGEC) is located in a prime area in the Cooch Behar District. The college is easily accessible to the students, as it is well-connected to any part of the city.

The college is accredited by AICTE and Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal (Formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology) – WBUT and Government of India and the Department of Higher Education, Government of West Bengal.

The Institute has its own wide campus of 20 acres with state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculties, and extensive computer facilities coupled with high-tech teaching-learning tools.

Cooch Behar Government Engineering College is also dedicated to playing an active role in local development initiatives and enhancing the well-being of the surrounding community.
